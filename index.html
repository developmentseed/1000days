Nutrition Interventions

This map provides a snapshot of how major U.S.-based non-governmental organizations are working to improve nutrition for mothers and children during the critical 1,000 day window between pregnancy and age two. The map highlights work being done in 27 countries* with high levels of child undernutrition in the context of socioeconomic data layers. The numbers indicate how many projects are underway in each country, based on the organizations surveyed. Click to zoom in for specific project locations and hover over markers for more information.
